# Support Deflector - Business Case

**Plugin:** nixtla-support-deflector
**Category:** Internal Efficiency
**Status:** Planned
**Last Updated:** 2025-12-15

---

## Problem Statement

Engineering teams at small AI companies like Nixtla spend 15-25% of their time on support tickets, many of which are repetitive questions answered in documentation. This creates a costly drain on resources that should be focused on product development.

---

## Target Customer

| User | Need |
|------|------|
| Engineering teams | Reduce time spent on repetitive support |
| Support staff | Faster, consistent responses |
| Nixtla leadership | Reclaim engineering productivity |

---

## Market Opportunity

- **Internal ROI**: Frees engineering capacity worth approximately $15-25K/month in recovered productivity for a 5-person team
- **Time savings**: 10+ hours/week of engineering time returned to product work
- **Support quality**: Consistent, documentation-backed responses

---

## ROI Calculation

| Metric | Current | With Plugin | Savings |
|--------|---------|-------------|---------|
| Avg response time | 4-8 hours | <1 hour | 50%+ reduction |
| Auto-resolved tickets | 0% | 30% | Engineering time saved |
| Engineering support hours/week | 15-25 hrs | 5-10 hrs | 10+ hrs/week |

---

## Competitive Positioning

No direct competitor offers AI-powered support deflection specifically tuned for Nixtla documentation and time-series forecasting context.

---

## Recommendation

**Priority:** Medium
**Effort:** 4-6 weeks for MVP with GitHub Issues integration
**Impact:** Indirect revenue through recovered engineering productivity
