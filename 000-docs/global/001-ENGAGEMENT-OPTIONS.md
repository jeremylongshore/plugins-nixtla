## How to Choose

### Choose Evaluate if...

- You want to see real value before committing
- You need internal buy-in from your team
- Budget approval requires demonstrated ROI
- You're not in a rush

### Choose Pilot if...

- You want one quick win to prove the concept
- You have a clear pain point (cost, support, onboarding)
- You prefer low-risk, incremental investments
- 4-6 weeks fits your timeline

### Choose Platform if...

- You see the strategic value of being a platform
- You want to differentiate from competitors (Prophet, ARIMA)
- You have budget for a comprehensive developer experience
- You're ready to invest in market expansion

A: Absolutely. That's the recommended path if you're risk-averse.

A: We can do another Pilot (one plugin at a time) or upgrade to Platform pricing for multi-plugin bundles.

A: Yes. The license is MIT. You own what we build together.

A: No problem. You keep access to the working plugins and documentation. No hard feelings.
